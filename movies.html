Jurassic Park (1993):A millionaire, John Hammond, creates a theme park featuring cloned dinosaurs. When security systems fail, visitors are trapped and must survive the dinosaurs that go haywire. The Lost World: Jurassic Park (1997):Four years later, a team is sent to a nearby island where dinosaurs live in freedom. They soon discover a plan to transport some dinosaurs to the city, resulting in chaos. Jurassic Park III (2001):A pair of parents trick Dr. Alan Grant into accompanying them to an island where they search for their missing son. Once again, the characters must face dangerous dinosaurs.
Jurassic World (2015):A new park, Jurassic World, opens on the same island with advanced technology. However, a genetically modified hybrid dinosaur escapes, wreaking havoc and forcing visitors to flee to survive. Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018):Following the collapse of Jurassic World, a group attempts to save the dinosaurs from a volcanic eruption. However, they uncover a plot to auction off the dinosaurs, leading to their release into the human world. Jurassic World: Dominion (2022):Dinosaurs now live among humans, creating a global conflict. The protagonists of both sagas unite to stop a greater threat derived from genetic manipulation and the loss of control of dinosaurs.

T-Rex:is one of the most iconic predators in the movies. It is massive, strong and ferocious, it is a constant threat, but it is also presented as an unwilling ally, fighting other predators. Velociraptors:Fast, smart, and deadly, Velociraptors are capable of coordinating team attacks. One of the raptors, Blue, forms a bond with Owen Grady, which humanizes these predators to some extent. Brachiosaurus:These giant herbivores are some of the first dinosaurs the characters see in Jurassic Park, representing the awe and wonder of cloning. They are calm and majestic, but their size makes them an imposing presence.
Spinosaurus:One of the most dangerous antagonists in the series, it is larger and stronger than the T-Rex. Its ability to swim and its agility make it an even greater threat. The Indominus Rex combines the DNA of several dinosaurs, making it extremely intelligent, powerful, and difficult to stop. Indoraptor is an even more advanced and deadly creation, designed to be a perfect predator.
